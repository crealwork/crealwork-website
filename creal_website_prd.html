# Project: CREAL One-Page Website ## Overview CREAL is a premier consulting firm at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Marketing. The goal is to create a high-converting, professional, and modern one-page website that showcases their expertise, services, and value proposition. ## Brand Identity - **Name:** CREAL - **Focus:** AI + Marketing Consulting - **Primary Accent Color:** #FFCC4E (Yellow/Gold) - **Visual Style:** Professional, tech-forward, clean, and high-end. Use of white space, bold typography, and subtle AI-themed visual elements. - **Logos:** Utilizing provided symbol and text logos (Yellow, White, Black versions). ## Core Sections (One-Page Flow) 1. **Hero Section:** High-impact headline, clear sub-headline about AI-driven marketing growth, and a primary CTA (e.g., "Schedule a Consultation"). 2. **About CREAL:** Brief overview of the firm's mission to bridge the gap between advanced AI technology and strategic marketing. 3. **Services:** - AI Strategy & Implementation - Predictive Analytics for Marketing - Automated Content Personalization - Performance Optimization 4. **The CREAL Edge (Value Prop):** Why choose them? (e.g., Expertise, ROI-focused, cutting-edge tech). 5. **Process:** A simple 3-4 step guide on how they work with clients (Assess -> Strategize -> Implement -> Scale). 6. **Testimonials/Social Proof:** Placeholder section for client success stories. 7. **Contact/CTA Footer:** Final push for conversion with a contact form or direct booking link. ## Design Requirements - **Device:** Desktop (responsive). - **Navigation:** Sticky header with anchor links to sections. - **Accent Color:** #FFCC4E used for CTAs, highlights, and key icons. - **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or Roboto). - **Imagery:** Use provided logos. Supplement with high-quality, abstract AI/Tech imagery.
